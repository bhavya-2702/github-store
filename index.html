ABOUT US

Elegant Collections is the go-to place for fashionable clothing made for women. We, DIOR STORE situated at 100 feet road, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, we aim to create a space for women to come together and share in their love of fashion. Our glamorous and comfortable clothing is just what you're looking for find your next favourite outfit in our amazing offerings.
